Mission

Tech it Out Philly teaches students how to use computer science in fun and meaningful applications. Together, we can build websites to change the world. Our students make websites that address the most pressing social issues of our time. Tech it Out Philly provides a safe space for students to explore CS with talented mentors to help along the way.

FAQ

Is it free?

Yes, it is! Totally free for all. Thanks to funding from the University of Pennsylvania.

What do I bring?

Nothing but your excitement. We'll provide computers, hardware, food and drinks, and SEPTA cards.

Do I need prior experience?

Not at all. We will teach all of our projects from start to finish, without any assumptions about participant's prior skill levels.

Who's eligible to sign up?

Any Philly high school (9th-12th grade) student is encouraged to sign up.
